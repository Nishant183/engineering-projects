    Ride Now with TAKUSHI


    You can book an Cab instantly from your Cab Website using RIDE NOW option.

    All you need to do is:-

    - Turn on your phone's Locations settings (Location or GPS) to make a booking.

    - Login to your Cab Website. On Logging in, you'll be shown Cabs that are near your location.
    Note-If you are a first time user, you will need to Create an Account with Cab to avail Cab services. To know more about Creating an Cab Account click here.

    -On Logging in, you'll be shown the Cabs available near your location.

    - Set up your pickup location and enter your drop location on Book your Ride screen by either moving the location pin on the map to your exact desired pickup/drop location or by selecting the exact address from the search bar.

    - Select a Cab type and then click RIDE NOW.

    - Tap CONFIRM to make your booking.

    You will receive a booking confirmation on your Website screen. You will be able to view your ride details, Cab location on the map, and the time your driver will take to pick you up on Pickup Arriving screen.

    You will also receive your ride details (Cab & driver details) through push notification.

    Drive with TAKUSHI


    To attach your car with Cab, you need the following documents:

    Mandatory Documents

    1. Vehicle Insurance
    2. RC Book
    3. PAN Card
    4. Address Proof
    5. Cancelled Cheque
    6. Commercial Driving License
    7. Optional Documents

    Optional Documents

    1. Tax Receipts
    2. Fitness Certificate
    3. PUC Certificate
    4. Permit Certificate

    Once you have secured the above documents, please visit the nearest Cab BD office for the next steps
